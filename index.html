FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need prior CAD experience?

No prior experience needed. The course starts from fundamentals and progressively builds up to advanced product design.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Every learner who completes the projects earns a hiMatrix Academy certificate of completion.

Is the course online?

Yes - the course is delivered fully online with live sessions, project reviews and mentor support.

What laptop do I need?

Onshape runs in your browser, so any laptop with a stable internet connection works - no expensive workstation required.

How long is the course?

The course runs across several weeks of guided project work. Exact duration will be shared with registered candidates ahead of the intake.
